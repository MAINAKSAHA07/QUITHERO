# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Adult smokers who have tried quitting before and keep relapsing — stressed professionals, social smokers, and habit smokers who still believe cigarettes give them something useful (relief, focus, confidence, a break). They land on smono.app before the app, often on mobile, looking for a method that does not feel like another willpower battle.

## Product Purpose

Smono is a 30-day psychological quit program (10 days to quit, 20 days of smoke-free support) that removes the desire to smoke — not just the cigarette — via CBT, trigger mapping, mindfulness, and relapse prevention. The marketing site must convert visitors into app onboarding with clarity and calm confidence.

## Brand Personality

Calm · honest · grounded

Voice: direct without lecturing; hopeful without hype; clinical psychology made human. Emotions: safety, relief, quiet determination — not shame or hustle energy.

## Anti-references

- Purple-gradient SaaS templates and dark-mode “AI wellness” chrome
- Fear-mongering quit ads (blackened lungs, guilt CTAs)
- Dense newspaper/editorial magazine layouts
- Feature-card grids with generic icon rows and empty marketing fluff

## Design Principles

1. Desire before discipline — lead with the psychological insight, not willpower.
2. One idea per fold — hero, journey, method, proof, price; never a dashboard dump above the fold.
3. Product visible — show the real app experience (mockups, journey phases), not abstract wellness stock.
4. Calm commitment — soft sky/peach palette and soft surfaces; CTAs stay clear and warm, never aggressive.
5. Mobile-first honesty — most visitors are on phones; structure and type must breathe on small screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve reduced-motion paths for canvas/animations. Keep body contrast ≥4.5:1 on tinted backgrounds. Support keyboard FAQ accordion and focus-visible states. Avoid medical claims that require clinical framing; include healthcare disclaimer where relevant.
