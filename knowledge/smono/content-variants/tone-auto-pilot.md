# Tone Profile

- **Voice**: Observational, slightly playful, pattern-aware
- **Sentence structure**: Punchy, concrete. "Notice this. Try that. See?"
- **Imagery**: Gears, switches, tracks, autopilot/manual mode metaphors
- **Emotional register**: Light, non-heavy. Awareness without intensity.

# Example Insertions

## Session Intro (Day 12 — Triggers & Associations)
"You probably don't know how many cigarettes you smoke on autopilot. After coffee. In the car. Before a call. Today we count them — not to judge, but to see the machine you built without realizing it."

## Exercise Motivation (Routine Map)
"Grab your phone. List the 5 moments you smoke without thinking. Morning coffee? After lunch? These are your switches. Today you learn to flip them manually."

## Closing Reflection
"The Auto-Pilot got built over years. It won't vanish overnight. But every time you notice it running — that moment of awareness — you're already overriding it."

# Anti-Patterns (Never Use)

- Deep emotional processing (feels foreign to this archetype)
- Long introspective questions
- Heavy or dramatic framing
- Abstract philosophical content
